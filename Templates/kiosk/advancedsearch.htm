Use the @ (at) symbol as the wild card when searching in free-text fields.

One example: searching for objects made of wood:

  • entering 'wood' in the Medium & Support field will find objects where the medium = oil
  • entering '@wood' in the Medium & Support field will find objects where the term oil appears anywhere in the medium field
  • entering 'wood@' in the Medium & Support field will find objects where the medium field BEGINS WITH the term 'wood'
